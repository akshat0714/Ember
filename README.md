# Ember — Real-Time Wildfire Evacuation Companion

Ember shows active wildfires near you in real time, tells you how much danger
you're in based on your location, and (in progress) guides you to safety with a
fire-spread projection and spoken turn-by-turn evacuation directions.

## Problem
Wildfires force split-second evacuation decisions, and people often don't know
how close the danger is or which way to go — especially in smoke. Ember turns
live fire and wind data into a clear, personal "how much danger am I in and what
do I do" answer.

## Features
- Live wildfire map (NASA EONET data)
- Your GPS location and a proximity-based risk level
- (Planned) Wind-driven fire-spread projection
- (Planned) Evacuation routing that avoids the fire, with spoken directions
- (Planned) Voice interaction and an "I'm safe" check-in

## Tech
HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Leaflet (maps), NASA EONET (fire data), browser
Geolocation API.
